overnight

Description

This blueberry French toast casserole is made the night before and baked in the morning for a very delicious holiday breakfast or brunch. It's bursting with fresh blueberries, and covered with a rich blueberry sauce to make it one-of-a-kind!

ingredients

  • 12 slices day-old bread, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Prepare casserole: Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 1/2 of the bread cubes in the dish and top with cream cheese cubes.
  3. Sprinkle blueberries over the cream cheese, then top with remaining bread cubes.
